Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

Do’s
- Think twice before posting
- Keep your profile clean and professional
- Use privacy settings properly
- Verify the other user and information before sharing
- Be respectful to others online and prevent any forms of cyberbullying

Don’ts
- Do not share personal details with unknown users and publicly
- Avoid posting content that may offend certain people or community
- Do not click unknown links/check for fraudulent activity
- Avoid unnecessary online arguments
- Do not share your location with strangers
