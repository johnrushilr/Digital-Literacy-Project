Prevention Checklist for Online Safety

Personal Safety
- Never share OTPs with strangers
- Always check for proper website URLs before entering your personal details
- Avoid clicking links those are found to be suspicious
- Use strong passwords (mix of upper and lowercases, numbers and symbols)
- Enable two-factor authentication for logins

Banking and UPI Safety
- Verify UPI ID before sending money
- Do not accept unknown payment requests
- Do not click any link that may claim to give you money
- Do not share your banking details with anybody else

Crime Reporting Entities
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline: 1930
